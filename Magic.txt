====== Magic ======
Created Monday 26 June 2017

* Rituals based on the alignment of heavenly bodies
* Create different effects depending on time of day
* Extensive spellbooks and research put into perfecting rituals by the [[Castes:Krosine|astronomers]]
* The [[Constellations|stars]] you're born under determine how effective your magic is at different things, and also who is strong and weak to your spells
