====== Krosine ======
Created Monday 26 June 2017

* Middle Caste
* Followers and observers of The Eye of Kros (the constellation that they worship)
* Combine the knowledge of the upper castes with the wisdom of the lower castes
* Core political caste of any city that they are in
	* Elect the Arcon/Arxis of the city
	* Usually the upper house of legislature and decision making, with the Azajul as the lower house of legislature and decision making
* Non-hereditary: people join the caste from vote
