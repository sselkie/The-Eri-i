====== Aurora ======
Created Saturday 24 June 2017

* Largest and most powerful city state in The Eri'i
* Center of the alliance/bloc known as the [[Aurora Accord]]
* [[Important People:Arxis Quasar|Arxis Quasar]] was the leader of this city
* Flag: black star on white background
* Top of city is a large observatory
	* Located on a mountain near the centre of the city
